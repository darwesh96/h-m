CREATIVE PALETTE
PAISLEY DAYS

Inspire your everyday wardrobe with dreamy scarf prints, floaty silhouettes, feminine knits and more elegant boho styles — as modelled by New York based artist Lexie Smith.

Lexie Smith is a New York based artist and baker, who puddle-jumps between sculpture, writing and everything in between. She’s best known for using bread as a way to explore and start conversations around community, politics, the environment and more. Learn more about Lexie:
@leche_smith
@bread_on_earth

How would you describe the collection?
Feminine, characteristically bohemian.

What’s your favourite piece?
The plaid jacket. I gravitated to it all day, putting it on between shots and wearing it like it was my own. This is likely because it's a classic piece, reminiscent of things I've had that have
brought me comfort. I could probably keep it on for the next three months.

Do you have any advice for someone looking to embrace their creativity in 2019?
Find time to dig into yourself, and to educate yourself. Input more than you output. Give the internet a break, linger in public spaces, find your idols, worship them, abandon them.

What's coming up for Lexie Smith this year?
Writing a series of conversational, narrative recipes; warmer climates; quiet insurrection, reading books; being neighbourly.
